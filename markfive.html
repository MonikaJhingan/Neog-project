Hello, this is Monika, I belong to the city of Nawabs, Lucknow, and the capital of Uttar Pradesh. Depicting a perfect blend of Hindu, Muslim, Sikh culture, it is the heart of art, dance, music, and famous cuisines. “Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam” which implies that “the world is one family”is what I believe in. This family comprises of human beings, and beings who can’t express themselves verbally and also our "Mother Nature". So, not only the humans that need help but also those who are non-verbal. So I call myself a “Zoophilist”. As animals are the purest souls on earth, they don't demand anything, they can only give their unconditional love and affection. So we, all should take care of our fellow beings and make this world a better place to live. Humanity has to think about global growth together with their personal growth as we need to protect our mother Nature from all sorts of damage and pave the way for happiness for our new ones.
Live and let live. Thanking you.
